Determination authority: execution admissibility

FlowSignal — Execution Boundary

FlowSignal evaluates whether bind-time conditions for execution are present. It does not prove upstream truth or future policy validity.

Observes / evaluates
  • Presented authority state at bind-time
  • Bind-time policy conditions
  • Delegation path resolution
  • Escalation conditions
  • Witness-state contract status where required
Does not determine
  • Whether upstream declarations are substantively correct
  • Whether the originating policy was the right policy
  • Whether another layer’s artifact is valid
  • Whether the same bind would be admissible under future policy

Vocabulary Discipline

Observation vs Determination Rule

Before a scenario fixture ships, run this mechanical pass across every layer panel.

CheckQuestionAction
1List the verbs used by the layer.Highlight words such as validates, certifies, proves, authorises.
2Is each verb observation or determination?Keep observation verbs for evidence-class surfaces.
3Does this layer hold authority over that question?If not, rewrite into observation vocabulary.
4Is cross-layer awareness being implied?Move it into reviewer view and stitch by correlation ID.
